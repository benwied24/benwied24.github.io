Ben's Final Slideshow - Poetry

I started out with retro computers...

But I concluded too quickly; it wasn't really what I wanted to do

So I started thinking...

And decided to compose a series of 10 poems.

And added effects and sounds to certain phrases and words

My Process

I started out by brainstorming each poem, before drafting it and editing it. After I was finished with all 10, I started to add effects to certain words/phrases, bracket-ing certain words to indicate that it had an effect.

My experiences

I learned alot about certain words that can be useful in my style of poetry. Sometimes, the poetry I was writing served as a sort of outlet for any bad emotions/thoughts I felt.

My findings

I found that, over the course of April (when I was writing), I became more and more proficient in the 3-4 stanza, 4 line structure of my poems.I also became more proficient with writing rhymes.
